My Story

I am Christopher Medeiros, an innovative Front-End Developer with a passion for creating responsive and user-friendly web applications. My journey in software development began with a commitment to continuous learning, culminating in an honors certificate from Amazon's Software Development Bootcamp and certifications from Meta and IBM. With experience in both freelance and collaborative environments, I have developed and maintained software applications using JavaScript and React.js. My time as a Software Developer Intern at Correlation One allowed me to deepen my understanding of modern software practices, where I actively participated in all phases of the project lifecycle, from critical problem-solving to testing and debugging. I pride myself on delivering cohesive solutions that enhance user experience and align with client goals. In addition to my technical skills, I bring strong soft skills to the table, including project management, attention to detail, and effective collaboration. My role as an Assistant Manager at Amazon honed my leadership abilities and analytical troubleshooting skills, allowing me to successfully manage a team and optimize operational processes. I am excited about leveraging my diverse skill set in technologies such as Node.js, SQL, and modern front-end frameworks, while continuously seeking opportunities for professional growth in fast-paced, solution-driven teams.
